Our Mission

At From&ForMada , we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to learn and grow in a supportive environment. We are dedicated to providing free coding education and mentorship to students in rural Madagascar.

What We Do

Intro to Coding

Teaching programming fundamentals to open doors to technology and creativity.

Building 2D Games

Students design and build their own games from scratch!

Post High School Guidance

Guiding students towards higher education and successful career paths.
